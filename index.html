What We See
The stories are there.
The system isn’t.
We looked at NewView’s social presence, website, and public communications. The mission is powerful. The content infrastructure doesn’t match it.
The team is posting consistently across four platforms — Facebook, LinkedIn, Instagram, and YouTube. The issue isn’t output. It’s that the posts aren’t driving the engagement, reach, and follower growth the stories deserve. There’s no unified strategy tying the platforms together, and no storytelling framework turning the best-performing post types (employee spotlights, client stories) into a repeatable engine.
The gap between NewView’s impact and its visible mission is the entire opportunity. There’s no bad reputation to overcome. Just a story the world hasn’t heard yet.
